# Neon Dash

Neon Dash is an interactive browser game where you jump, double-jump, collect coins, grab power-ups, and survive as long as possible.

## How to Play

- Press **Space** or **Up Arrow** to jump.
- Click or tap the game to jump.
- Press **P** or the **Pause** button to pause.
- You can double-jump before landing.
- Avoid red blocks and purple drones.
- Collect yellow coins for extra points.
- Grab blue shield power-ups to block one hit.
- Grab pink plus power-ups to gain a life.
- Press **Restart** to start over.
- Your best score is saved in your browser.
